Who the HELL is Eichi Tenshouin, anyway?

I'm glad you asked!

Eichi Tenshouin is the primary antagonist of the first—and technically most famous—era of the mobile game franchise Ensemble Stars!! He is the heir to the Tenshouin Conglomerate and chronically ill, yet still loves idols and aspires to be one, and make an ideal world for them. He is now the leader of fine and the representative of STARMAKER PRODUCTIONS, an idol agency of his own making. Due to his previous status as an antagonist and the general insane way in which Enstars!! characters talk, he's very often perceived negatively. That's why I've founded Eichi Defenders Incorporated, to defend my best and most loveliest boy.
